Who am I?

JUDE BRITTON

I am currently studying Mathematical Physics at Nottingham University.
Astronomer of the year, Physicist of the year.
I have also performed with other local artists including: 'Andy B Goode' (1940s, 1950s, 1960s Rock and Roll / Rat Pack Singer), Sam (Four Legs Good), Nath Brooks.
I was part of a local song writers development project with professional artists from Make Some Noise, Positive Labels, Sinewave Studeos and Tamworth Council.
Later this year I am going back into the studio to record some of my songs for radio play.
Notable recent performances include:

  • Lichfield Festival

  • Tamworth Christmas lights
  • Tamworth Bandstand
  • Church Gresley Bandstand

  • A regular at local open mic events
