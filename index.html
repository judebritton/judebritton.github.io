Who am I?

JUDE BRITTON

Originally from Tamworth, I am currently studying Mathematical Physics at Nottingham University.
I was part of a local song writers development project with professional artists from Make Some Noise, Positive Labels, The Song Lab and Sinewave Studeos.
I have also performed with other local artists including: 'Andy B Goode' (1940s, 1950s, 1960s Rock and Roll / Rat Pack Singer), Sam Dettmer (Four Legs Good) and Nath Brooks.
Later this year I am going back into The Song Lab studio to record some of my songs for radio play.
Notable recent performances include:

  • Loughborough Youth Sports Games Opening Ceremony (3000 people)
  • Lichfield Festival
  • TCR FM (Tamworth radio)
  • Tamworth Christmas lights
  • Tamworth Bandstand
  • Tamworth Eats and Beats Food Festival
  • Church Gresley Bandstand
  • Festival of Leisure
  • Various Pubs
  • A regular at local open mic events near Tamworth and Nottingham
